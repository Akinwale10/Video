## COLOR PIPELINE

### ACES Workflow

**Input Transform (IDT)**
- ARRI ALEXA 65: ALEXA Wide Gamut → ACEScct

**Working Color Space**
- ACEScct (logarithmic encoding)
- Maintains wide gamut through VFX pipeline

**Look Development**
- Custom LUT: Teal shadows, orange highlights
- Applied in ACEScct color space
- Split-tone curves:
  - Shadows: RGB(20, 35, 40) → Teal
  - Highlights: RGB(255, 180, 100) → Orange

**Output Transforms (ODT)**
- Rec.709 (HD/YouTube delivery)
- P3-D65 (DCI projector)
- Rec.2020 (HDR delivery, future-proofing)

### Color Grading Tools

**Primary Corrections**
- Lift/Gamma/Gain
- Contrast: Crushed blacks (lift to 8 IRE)
- Saturation: -20% global, preserve skin tones

**Secondary Corrections**
- HSL qualifier for blood (boost crimson)
- Power windows for creature (edge sharpening)
- Vignette: Subtle, -0.3 stops at edges

**Film Grain**
- 35mm grain emulation
- Intensity: 10% (subtle texture)

## SAFETY & COMPLIANCE

### Electrical Safety

**Certifications Required**
- Licensed electrician (on-set at all times)
- High-voltage certification for Tesla coil operation

**Equipment**
- GFI protection on all water-exposed circuits
- Grounding verified before each setup
- Fire extinguishers: Class C (electrical) x3

### Prosthetics Safety

**Medical Considerations**
- Allergy test: Silicone patch test 48 hours before
- Cooling breaks: Every 90 minutes
- Medical professional on-call
- EpiPen on set (allergic reaction protocol)

### Rain Rig Safety

**Precautions**
- Non-slip mats in all wet areas
- Drainage verified before first water release
- All electrical 20+ feet from water
- Spotter assigned to monitor water levels
