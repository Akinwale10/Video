# FRANKENSTEIN: FIRST BREATH
**A 2-Minute Cinematic Short**

---

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

**Genre:** Gothic Horror / Body Horror  
**Runtime:** 2:00 minutes  
**Aspect Ratio:** 2.39:1 (Anamorphic)  
**Color Grade:** Cold teal/orange split-tone, high contrast, crushed blacks  
**Camera System:** ARRI ALEXA 65 + Panavision Sphero 65 lenses  
**Frame Rate:** Native 24fps / 48fps for slow-motion lightning inserts  
**Sound Mix:** Dolby Atmos 7.1.4  
**Format:** 8K RAW (ARRIRAW)

---

## SYNOPSIS

In the heart of a storm-ravaged castle laboratory, Dr. Victor Frankenstein witnesses the impossible: his creation draws its first breath. As lightning tears through the night sky, electricity courses through the creature's assembled body, awakening consciousness where there should be none. In two visceral minutes, we witness the birth of a monster and the terror of its creator—a moment where science transcends morality, and life emerges from death.

---

## VISUAL STYLE

**Color Palette:**
- Cold teal shadows (graded to 5500K)
- Warm orange highlights on skin tones (3200K)
- Crushed blacks for maximum contrast
- Desaturated midtones except for blood (deep crimson)

**Camera Language:**
- Static, locked-off shots for moments of dread
- Handheld during Victor's panic
- Slow push-ins for emotional revelations
- Macro photography for body horror details

**Lighting:**
- Motivated by practical sources (lightning, candles, Jacob's ladder)
- Hard sidelight for dramatic shadows
- Rim lighting on creature to separate from background
- Strobe effects for lightning strikes

## SOUND DESIGN (DOLBY ATMOS 7.1.4)

### Channel Layout
- **L/C/R:** Primary dialogue and frontal effects
- **Surround (4):** Ambient thunder, laboratory atmosphere
- **Height (4):** Lightning cracks, ceiling sparks
- **LFE:** Sub-bass hits, thunder impacts, heartbeat

### Sound Layers

| Element | Source Material | Processing | Placement |
|---------|----------------|------------|-----------|
| **Thunder** | Recorded storm + synthesis | 3D panned (L→R→Surround), LFE hits at 30Hz | Overhead/surround speakers |
| **Heartbeat** | Sub-kick sample + real human pulse (stethoscope) | Ramping tempo: 80 BPM → 120 BPM | LFE + center channel |
| **Creature Voice** | Actor performance + grizzly bear growl + pitch shift (-30%) | Heavy reverb: "Stone Room" preset, long decay | Center + surround spread |
| **Electrical Sparks** | Tesla coil recording + metal scrape | High-frequency emphasis, panned across ceiling | Height channels |
| **Laboratory Ambience** | Wind, rain, creaking wood | Low-pass filtered at 8kHz | Surround channels |
| **Violin** | Solo cello bow on violin string | Detuned 50 cents, delay tail (2.5s) | Center with surround reverb |

### Key Audio Moments
- **0:12** - Thunder + LFE hit synchronized with lightning strike
- **0:20** - Heartbeat enters, growing in intensity
- **0:28** - Thunder ROAR with massive LFE (peak level)
- **1:14** - Violin sustain begins, eerie and detuned

3. **Assembly Cut:** Day 1-2
4. **Fine Cut:** Day 3-5
5. **VFX Turnover:** After locked cut
6. **Sound Design:** Parallel to VFX
7. **Online/Conform:** After VFX delivery

### Color Grading
1. **Workflow:** ACEScct color space
2. **LUT:** Custom teal/orange split-tone
3. **Deliverables:** Rec.709 (HD), P3 (DCP), Rec.2020 (HDR)
4. **Style Notes:**
   - Shadows: Teal (RGB: 20, 35, 40)
   - Highlights: Orange (RGB: 255, 180, 100)
   - Skin tones: Preserve but desaturate 20%
   - Blacks: Crushed to 8 IRE
   - Contrast: 1.4:1 ratio

### VFX Production
1. **Tracking:** 3D camera tracking in all VFX shots
2. **Lighting:** HDR environment maps captured on set
3. **Review:** Daily cineSync sessions
4. **Renders:** EXR sequences, 8K resolution
5. **Software Stack:** Nuke, Houdini, EmberGen, Mari

### Sound Design & Mix
1. **Software:** Pro Tools Ultimate + Dolby Atmos Renderer
2. **Session:** 96kHz / 32-bit float
3. **Stem Delivery:**
   - Dialogue stem
   - Music stem
   - SFX stem
   - Atmos master
