## LIGHTING SETUPS

### Setup A: "Storm Laboratory" (Shots 002-010)
**Key Light:** ARRI M40 through window (moonlight simulation)
**Fill:** SkyPanel S360 bounced (cool teal, 5500K)
**Practical:** Tesla coil, candles (12-15 units)
**Effects:** Astera Tubes for lightning flicker
**Ratios:** 4:1 (high contrast)

### Setup B: "Creature Rising" (Shots 011-014)
**Key Light:** SkyPanel from above (God light)
**Edge:** M40 with 1/2 CTO (warm rim on creature)
**Fill:** Minimal, let shadows play
**Practical:** Candles, dying electrical sparks
**Ratios:** 6:1 (dramatic)

### Setup C: "Aftermath" (Shots 015-016)
**Key Light:** Practical storm through door
**Fill:** Bounce card (white, minimal)
**Effects:** Rain backlight with M40
**Ratios:** Natural, motivated by storm

## SPECIAL EFFECTS COORDINATION

### Rain Rig Operation
**Shots Required:** 001, 015, background for multiple interior shots
**Rig Specs:** 1000 GPM, 12 spray bars
**Coverage:** 40' x 30' area
**Drainage:** 4" PVC to exterior
**Safety:** GFI protected circuits, non-slip mats
**Personnel:** 2 SFX crew for operation

### Electrical Effects
**Shots Required:** 004, 008, background for Act I
**Equipment:** Tesla coil (15kV), Jacob's ladder, practical spark generator
**Safety Zone:** 10-foot perimeter, marked with tape
**Personnel:** Licensed electrician + SFX supervisor
**Fire Safety:** Class C extinguishers (x3) positioned
**Power Requirements:** 220V, 60A circuit

### Breakaway Floor
**Shot Required:** 013 (creature crashes to floor)
**Construction:** Pre-scored boards, balsa wood reinforcement
**Reset Time:** 45 minutes
**Coverage:** 6' x 4' section
**Safety:** Stunt coordinator on set, pad underneath
**Takes Budgeted:** 3 maximum
